My team and I were the local winners of the 7th edition of the Cassini Hackathon, the first time it was held in Albania. In just 48 hours, we developed a website that utilized Copernicus and Galileo datasets to provide users with insights into the most suitable crops to grow in specific fields. Robotech winners

Participating in the RoboTech competition, organized by the Metropolitan University of Tirana and COD, our team proudly secured 1st place, alongside winning the public prize and the pyramid prize. We developed RoboGuide, an innovative robotic guide designed to enhance the tourist experience in museums and various locations. The project involved utilizing Arduino for hardware control and coding in C++ which was my main role, while also integrating Raspberry Pi and voice boxes to provide interactive guidance. Our success garnered national attention, resulting in several interviews, which highlighted our achievement and the potential of RoboGuide in the field of tourism.

Human Rights Academy

Participating in the Human Rights Academy, a one-week camp organized by YVNO and YPEERS, was an enriching experience that deepened my understanding of human rights issues. This program not only provided valuable training but also includes ongoing follow-ups throughout the year, emphasizing the importance of continuous engagement and advocacy.
